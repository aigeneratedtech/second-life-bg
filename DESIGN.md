# Design System - Second Life (Втори Живот)

## Aesthetic
"Warm & Trustworthy" - Evoking feelings of care, nature, and safety.

## Color Palette
- **Primary (Sage Green)**: `#849b87`
- **Secondary (Beige/Cream)**: `#f4f1ea`
- **Accent (Deep Earth)**: `#5e6b5f`
- **Background**: `#ffffff` (White) & `#f9f8f6` (Off-white for sections)
- **Text**: `#2c332d` (Dark Green-Grey)

## Typography
- **Headings**: Serif or Soft Sans-serif (e.g., 'Merriweather', 'Georgia', or rounded 'Nunito').
- **Body**: Clean Sans-serif (e.g., 'Open Sans', 'Helvetica Neue', 'Arial').

## UI Elements
- **Buttons**:
  - Primary: Solid Sage Green, White Text, Rounded Corners.
  - Secondary/Outline: Transparent, Sage Green Border, Sage Green Text.
- **Cards**:
  - White background, subtle shadow, rounded corners (8px-12px).
